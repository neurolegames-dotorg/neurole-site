Prefrontal Cortex

Executive function, decision-making, impulse control. Implicated in ADHD, depression, and schizophrenia.

Amygdala

Fear and emotional memory. Central to anxiety disorders, PTSD, and phobias.

Hippocampus

Memory formation and spatial navigation. Severely affected in Alzheimer's and depression.

Anterior Cingulate Cortex

Error detection and conflict monitoring. Involved in OCD and mood disorders.

Insula

Interoception and emotional awareness. Plays a role in addiction and eating disorders.

Ventral Striatum

Reward anticipation and motivation. Core to addiction, depression, and anhedonia.

Orbitofrontal Cortex

Evaluates reward and consequence. Disrupted in OCD and impulse-control disorders.

Hypothalamus

Regulates stress hormones, sleep, appetite. Central to mood and anxiety disorders.
