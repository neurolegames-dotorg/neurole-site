Volunteer with Neurole

Neurole is built by neuroscience students, clinicians, and educators who think learning the nervous system should feel like a game, not a grind. Here's where we could use your brain.

Write daily cases

Have clinical or neuroscience training? Draft new patient scenarios and symptom progressions for The Daily Case.

Curate brain images

Help source and label MRI/diagram images and answer choices for Map the Brain.

Fact-check fun facts

Find and verify a peer-reviewed study behind each week's neuroscience fun fact.

Build & design

Engineers and designers welcome — help us ship new game modes and polish the experience.

Media & outreach

Help grow our audience — create short-form content, manage social media, write newsletters, or represent Neurole at neuroscience events and university clubs.

📲 Media Management

We're looking for people to help manage and grow Neurole's online presence across platforms. Tasks include:

  • Creating and scheduling Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok content about neuroscience
  • Designing graphics and short explainer videos based on our game cases
  • Writing and sending newsletters or blog posts
  • Coordinating partnerships with neuroscience clubs, premed societies, and student organisations
  • Monitoring engagement and suggesting content based on what our players respond to

Ideal for: communications, marketing, journalism, or neuroscience students with a presence on social media.
